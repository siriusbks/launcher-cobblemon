RAM Allocation
This will let you allocate more or less ram to your game. More RAM means the game will run better and you will get higher performance. 4GB is recommended for most systems.

However, if you allocate more RAM that your computer has, your computer may crash.

It is recommended that you do not allocate more than 50% of your avaliable RAM.

- Maximum RAM: This is the maximum amount you want to allocate to your computer. It is strongly recommended that you do not allocate more than 50% of your avaliable RAM.

- Minimum RAM: This is the minimum amount of RAM you wish to allocate.
